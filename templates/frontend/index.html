We deliver landmark projects

Food for All International is a Not-For-Profit Agricultural Development, Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) set up to support farmers, enhance agricultural production and help improve food security and alleviate poverty. FFAI was established in 2002 and is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission, Abuja. Today FFAI has worked with farmers in almost all parts of the Niger Delta addressing critical components in the agricultural sector, strengthening agricultural productivity.

Our Vision

Establishment of alternative economy in the oil-rich Niger delta region of Nigeria through sustainable Agricultural development and commercialization. Niger Delta is a region where poverty stares all in the face in the midst of seeming plenty, and opportunities are few in the oil industry, the alternative is Agriculture.

Our Mission

To improve the quality of life for rural farmers through the production, multiplication and distribution of high quality seeds, and also through the transfer of proven suitable and adaptable Agricultural technology in the Niger delta region of Nigeria and beyond.

Our Trademark

Service to farmers in remote and difficult environment.

Our Strategy

Partnership with private individuals, corporate bodies, specialized agencies including research bodies and international agencies.
